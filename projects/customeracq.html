Customer Acquisition Cost Estimation Model

Situation

The Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Estimation Model project focused on developing a model to accurately predict the cost of acquiring new customers. This was crucial for optimizing marketing budgets and enhancing ROI on customer acquisition strategies.

Task

The task was to create a robust estimation model that could estimate the CAC based on various business metrics and customer data. This involved analyzing historical data, applying statistical techniques, and validating the model's predictive accuracy.

Action

We employed a range of statistical methods and machine learning algorithms to build and refine the CAC estimation model. Key actions included data aggregation, feature selection, and model training to ensure accurate and reliable cost predictions.

Result

  • Developed an accurate CAC estimation model that provides reliable predictions for customer acquisition costs.
  • Enabled the client to optimize marketing spend and improve ROI on customer acquisition.
  • Delivered actionable insights through a comprehensive dashboard, aiding in strategic decision-making and budget allocation.
