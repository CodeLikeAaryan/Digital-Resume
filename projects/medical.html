Medical Report Summarizer

Project Overview

The Medical Report Summarizer project was designed to streamline the handling of medical reports by extracting key information, summarizing the content, and converting it into audio format. Leveraging advanced language models (LLMs) and text-to-speech technology, this tool provides an accessible and efficient way to interpret medical documents.

Objective

The aim was to create a system that can extract pertinent information from medical reports, generate concise summaries, and convert these summaries into audio format. This enables users, including healthcare professionals and patients, to easily access and understand medical information.

Approach

We implemented a multi-step process involving:

  • Text Extraction: Extracting text from medical reports using OCR technology.
  • Summarization: Utilizing LLMs to generate concise summaries of the extracted text.
  • Text-to-Speech Conversion: Converting the summaries into audio format for easy listening.

Results

  • Developed a robust extraction system capable of accurately capturing information from various types of medical reports.
  • Created a high-quality summarization model that provides clear and concise summaries of complex medical content.
  • Integrated a text-to-speech module that offers audio outputs for improved accessibility and usability.
