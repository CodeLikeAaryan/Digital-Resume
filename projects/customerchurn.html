Customer Churn Prediction

Situation

Understanding customer behavior is crucial for maintaining competitive advantage. Our project focuses on predicting customer churn—identifying those at risk of leaving—using sophisticated analytical models to enable proactive retention strategies.

Task

The core task was to analyze customer data and develop a model capable of predicting churn with high accuracy. This involved exploring various data attributes, applying machine learning techniques, and validating the model to ensure robust predictions.

Action

We utilized a combination of machine learning algorithms, including logistic regression and decision trees, to build and refine our churn prediction model. The process involved data preprocessing, feature engineering, and iterative model tuning to enhance performance.

Result

  • Achieved a model accuracy of over 90% , significantly improving our ability to predict customer churn.
  • Provided actionable insights leading to a strategic reduction in churn rates by 25% through targeted retention efforts.
  • Developed an interactive dashboard that visualizes key metrics and predictions, aiding in data-driven decision-making for stakeholders.
