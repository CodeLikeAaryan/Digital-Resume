Restaurant Review Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview

The Restaurant Review Analysis project aimed to gain a deep understanding of customer feedback by analyzing reviews from various restaurants. Using advanced data analytics, we transformed raw review data into actionable insights to enhance customer satisfaction and business performance.

Objective

The primary objective was to develop a comprehensive analysis dashboard that visualizes key metrics and trends in customer reviews. This includes assessing customer sentiment, identifying common issues, and highlighting high-performing restaurants based on review data.

Approach

Our approach included:

  • Data Aggregation: Collecting reviews from multiple sources and consolidating them into a unified dataset.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Applying text mining techniques to gauge customer sentiment and extract key themes.
  • Visualization: Designing interactive dashboards in PowerBI to display trends, customer feedback, and performance metrics.
  • Insight Generation: Identifying actionable insights to improve service quality and enhance customer experience.

Results

  • Delivered an interactive dashboard that provides real-time insights into customer feedback and review trends.
  • Highlighted common issues and areas for improvement across different restaurants, leading to targeted improvements.
  • Enabled restaurant owners to track performance and make data-driven decisions to enhance customer satisfaction.
