Credit Card Customer Transaction Anomaly Detection

Situation

The Credit Card Customer Transaction Anomaly Detection project aimed to identify unusual patterns and fraudulent activities in financial transactions. The challenge was to develop a proof of concept (POC) to demonstrate the efficacy of anomaly detection methods.

Task

The primary task was to create a POC system for detecting anomalies in customer transactions. This involved applying advanced algorithms to analyze transaction data, identify outliers, and generate actionable insights to enhance fraud prevention strategies.

Action

We utilized a variety of anomaly detection techniques, including statistical methods and machine learning models, to build the POC. Key steps included data preprocessing, feature extraction, and model training to effectively spot irregularities.

Result

  • Developed a functional POC that successfully detected anomalies in transaction data with a high degree of accuracy.
  • Highlighted potential fraudulent transactions and provided insights into fraudulent behavior patterns.
  • Demonstrated the value of anomaly detection techniques in enhancing fraud prevention and risk management strategies.
